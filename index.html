Meet Ralph Valdes

I Spent 40 Years Watching Marriages Die. Now I Spend My Life Helping Them Live.

Ralph Valdes spent four decades as a family-law attorney — sitting across from couples whose marriages were ending. He saw the same patterns over and over. He saw what happened when people stopped giving their best to God, to each other, and to their families. So he retired his law license — and picked up a pen.

He wrote five books. He became a counselor. He became a speaker. Not because he had all the answers — but because he had sat with too many people who ran out of time to find them. His message is simple: God gave His life for us. The least we can do is give Him our best.
